The Notebook

There is nothing more beautiful than a love story that was never supposed to happen. A young man named Noah falls in love with this beautiful girl named Allie. She comes from a wealthy family with high social standards. Having protective parents, Allie has been sent away to prestigious school. Meanwhile, Noah writes her letters that never reach her hands. Years later, the couple meets again and the love that once was there, is born again.

The story about their love is retold by Noah himself in the old age, when Allie is in the nursing-home suffering from a memory-loss.
